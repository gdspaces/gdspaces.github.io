Selected Answer: D
When you assign the Azure Active Directory Premium Plan 2 license to Group1, all the members of Group1 are assigned the license. As Group2 is a member of Group1, all members of Group2 will also be assigned the license. Similarly, Group3 is a member of Group2 and all members of Group3 will also be assigned the license. So all users User1, User2, User3, and User4 will be assigned the license. So the correct answer is D. User1, User2, User3, and User4.
