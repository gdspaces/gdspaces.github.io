Correct 1 - storage1, storage2, storage3 Lifecycle management policies are supported for block blobs and append blobs in general-purpose v2, premium block blob, and Blob Storage accounts. 2 - storage2 Only storage accounts that are configured for LRS, GRS, or RA-GRS support moving blobs to the archive tier.
