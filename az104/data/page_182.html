This might help those thinking is it 0 or 1 for the immutable policy - it is a max of two of these full stop, but depends if you have versioning turned on at the storage account level or not which dictates how many of the two immutable policies you can create at the container level (1 legal and 1 time based retention policy - legal hold is greyed out at the container level if versioning is set at the storage account level). https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/storage/blobs/immutable-legal-hold-overview. The answers to this test question are 3 (total of 5 allowed) for the first box and 1 (total of 2 allowed) for the second box
