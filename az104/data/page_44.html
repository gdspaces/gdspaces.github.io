Group2 is a member of Group1, and access reviews are set up for Group1, the reviewers will see a list of all members of Group1, including Group2 and its guest members. The reviewers can then review the access rights and permissions granted to each guest member of Group2 and decide whether to remove or adjust their access to specific resources.
