The answer is correct. - Deploying an app to a slot first(Test is this case) and swapping it into production makes sure that all instances of the slot are warmed up before being swapped into production. - After a swap, the slot with previously staged app now has the previous production app. If the changes swapped into the production slot aren't as you expect, you can perform the same swap immediately to get your "last known good site" back.
